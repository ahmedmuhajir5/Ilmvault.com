About IlmVault

A free, open, and beautiful digital library dedicated to preserving and sharing the Prophetic traditions for every Muslim, everywhere.

Our Mission

IlmVault was built to provide a clean, distraction-free experience for reading and studying hadith. We believe that sacred knowledge should be accessible to everyone — without ads, without clutter, without barriers.

The platform supports all major hadith collections, with authentic Arabic text, reliable English translations, narrator information, and scholarly grading.

Technology

Built with Next.js (App Router), PostgreSQL with full-text search indexing, and a Node.js API layer. The frontend is designed with an Arabic-first typography system using the Amiri typeface, optimized for readability in both RTL and LTR layouts.

All data is served via server-side rendering for instant loading and full SEO coverage. Search is powered by PostgreSQL's tsvector/tsquery with fallback fuzzy matching.
